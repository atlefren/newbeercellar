New BeerCellar

Tired of using a spreadsheet to keep tracks of what beers you have stored? Well, this app aims to remedy that, by letting you register your beers online, accessible everywhere!. (that is: as soon as I make this site mobile-friendly..)

Using a RateBeer dump, you can search through a butload of beers and breweries to add to your cellar.

Features

  • Add beers from ratebeer
  • Store a variety of attributes
  • Search and filter
